About the Project

What is CRAG?

CRAG stands for Cloud-Powered Corrective Retrieval-Augmented Generation. It's an advanced AI framework meticulously engineered to deliver information that is demonstrably more accurate, reliable, and context-aware than conventional models.

(C) Cloud

We leverage the scalability, reliability, and immense computational power of cloud infrastructure. This allows our system to handle massive datasets and complex queries without missing a beat.

(RAG) Retrieval

Like standard RAG, we first retrieve relevant information from a vast, curated knowledge base before attempting to generate a response. This grounds the AI in factual data.

(Corrective) C-RAG

This is our secret sauce. CRAG integrates a self-correction loop. It assesses the relevance of retrieved documents and, if they're not good enough, it actively refines the query and searches again. This corrective step significantly reduces errors and "hallucinations," ensuring the final answer is built on the most accurate information.
